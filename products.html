A classic favorite, Cappuccino combines a shot of espresso with a sprinkle of cinnamon, creating a delightful balance of flavors. Waffles

Classic waffles are a beloved breakfast or brunch treat. They are made from a batter consisting of flour, eggs, milk, sugar, and butter, which is cooked in a waffle iron to create a distinctive grid-like pattern. The result is a deliciously fluffy and slightly crisp waffle with a golden-brown exterior. Classic waffles are often served with a variety of toppings such as maple syrup, fresh fruits, whipped cream, or melted butter to enhance their flavor.

Cinnamon

Cinnamon rolls are a delightful sweet pastry enjoyed for breakfast or as a tasty treat any time of the day. They are made from a rich, yeasted dough that is rolled out into a rectangle, generously spread with a mixture of cinnamon, sugar, and butter, and then rolled tightly into a log shape.

Carbonara

Carbonara is a classic Italian pasta dish known for its rich and creamy sauce. It traditionally consists of spaghetti pasta tossed with a sauce made from eggs, grated cheese (usually Pecorino Romano or Parmesan), pancetta or bacon, black pepper, and sometimes cream. The hot pasta cooks the eggs slightly, creating a luscious and silky sauce.
